Welcome to our site

Welcome to a destination with focus on nature. Sagres is a town unlike other in Portugal, being situated in the extreme west of Portugal it is a harbor for people who want to escape to Nature. We have some of the most beautiful beaches in Portugal and we offer diving the year around. We offer both entry level courses and more advanced technical courses. Our team is able to cater to everyone's needs. We have easy acces to wrecks and cave diving.

Our dive center is situated in the harbor of sagres footsteps away from the sea.

Feel free to contact us if you have any Questions.

Diving in Sagres

Diving in Sagres is an exciting exploration into the ocean.

We offer a various types of diving on all levels wether your beginner or up to a new challange. We go out everyday for fundiving. As we set high standards for safety a Dive guide or instructor from our center will always be joing the dives.
